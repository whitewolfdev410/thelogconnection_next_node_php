A classic estate home with sweeping wrap around porches and a many-dormered roof, this design features a spacious, open main floor with vaulted living room.  
The L-shaped plan of the kitchen flows into the adjoining dining room. 
The master suite, conveniently located on the main floor, has a large ensuite and twin walk-in closets. 
Upstairs, 2 large bedrooms with their own full baths are joined by a catwalk that overlooks the foyer and living areas.
