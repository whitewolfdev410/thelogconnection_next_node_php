A soaring roof, numerous bay windows, and a variety of natural materials evoke echoes of Craftsman style and grand mountain retreats. 
The sprawling main floor includes generously sized living, dining, and kitchen areas, plus a sun room, garage, and two bedrooms with their own ensuite baths.
The upper floor is the domain of the master suite, with a luxurious ensuite bathroom, walk-in closet, storage room, loft, and  balconies.
