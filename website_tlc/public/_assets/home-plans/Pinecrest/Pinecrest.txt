This is a traditional log home style, combined with modern design techniques to create a fresh approach for a panoramic site. 
The stunning view from the upper library and loft areas into the rooms below is breathtaking. 
A central fireplace backing an extensive kitchen and dining area accents the main floor great room.  
The luxurious master suite features a walk-in closet and its own fireplace. 
An eclectic design that is both elegant and enticing.
