An affordable cottage with a luxurious feel.  
A spacious, soaring living room opens onto a large sundeck through a bank of patio doors.  
The L-shaped kitchen features a large island with a raised bar, plus an extra-large pantry. 
The upstairs master suite features a full ensuite bathroom and walk-in closet. 
An adjoining loft looks across the vaulted living area to large upper view windows.
