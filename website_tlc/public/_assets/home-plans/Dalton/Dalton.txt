In this combination of a traditional log home and modern open planning, main living space is centered in the home with bedrooms located in the side wings. 
The master suite features a full bathroom and a raised bay window. 
An open kitchen-dining area looks past the stairway into the large living room. 
French doors from many rooms lead to multiple outdoor decks for enjoying sun and fresh air. 
Upstairs, a cozy loft provides a private retreat.
